Drew S. DiSilvestro Jr.

Chief Executive Officer

Drew DiSilvestro is the President of AmeriTech Contracting and the driving force behind the company's growth and energy. He's known for blending big-picture vision with roll-up-your-sleeves leadership — keeping projects moving, people supported, and standards high.

Drew spent years managing complex acquisition and deployment programs for U.S. and international partners and later founded a construction firm in Connecticut that quickly grew past $4M in annual revenue. Before that, he served as a Senior Non-Commissioned Officer and Contracting Officer Representative in the U.S. Air Force, where his detail-oriented approach earned him a reputation as one of the best in his field.

At AmeriTech, Drew has helped expand the company from one small office to a nationwide federal contracting firm completing more than $165M in projects and counting.

Off the job, you'll find Drew on a golf course, at the gym, cheering on a game, or hanging out with his wife, Andrea, and their two boys, Johnny and Aedan. He works hard, plays hard, and brings that same spirit to AmeriTech every day.

Education:

  • EMBA, University of New Haven
  • BA in Business & Communications, Eastern Connecticut State University
  • AAS in Construction Technology & Management, Community College of the Air Force
  • Graduate, Entrepreneurship Bootcamp for Veterans, UConn School of Business
