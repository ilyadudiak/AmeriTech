How We Began

Our Roots

Founded in 2020 by Service-Disabled Veteran Scott Dahl, AmeriTech Contracting began as AmeriTech Mechanical with a simple yet powerful purpose: to make a meaningful impact for clients, partners, and communities. Scott’s unwavering determination, hands-on leadership, and commitment to excellence set the foundation for a company built on integrity and trust.

From day one, Scott has championed opportunities for fellow veterans and fostered a workplace culture rooted in diversity, inclusion, and respect. His drive to serve extends beyond business—it’s about empowering people, supporting communities, and always striving to do what’s right. Today, AmeriTech Contracting continues to grow, guided by the values that Scott instilled: service, dedication, and a passion for helping others succeed.

At AmeriTech, we are committed to helping our employees grow, building strong teams and leaders who take pride in their work and their impact.
