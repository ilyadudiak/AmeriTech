Michael Trinks

Chief Financial Officer

Michael Trinks is Chief Financial Officer at AmeriTech Contracting, overseeing financial planning, reporting, and the fiscal discipline that supports sustainable growth.

Michael partners with leadership to align budgets, forecasts, and compliance with the demands of federal contracting and complex project delivery.

His analytical approach and transparent stewardship help AmeriTech make sound decisions while investing in people, capabilities, and long-term success.
