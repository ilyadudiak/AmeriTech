Josh DiSilvestro

Director of Human Relations

Josh DiSilvestro leads Human Relations at AmeriTech Contracting, supporting recruiting, employee development, and a culture built on accountability and respect.

Josh works closely with leadership and project teams to strengthen communication, resolve challenges, and create opportunities for people to grow their careers.

His focus on people-first leadership helps AmeriTech attract, retain, and develop the talent behind every successful mission.
